Hex Editing

When you press E on the Hex View you enter editing mode. Press the arrow keys to move the cursor and type 0-9 or A-F to enter hex values. Press enter when you are ready to save and type Y. Press F8 to undo current changes. CTRL-V pastes in hex values from the clipboard. TAB switches between hex and ascii modes (not implemented yet). Press F1 to show this help and ESC to cancel editing.
